DAY 1

Algorithm Example: Making a Sandwich ü•™

Algorithm Steps:
1. Get bread slices
2. Open peanut butter jar
3. Spread peanut butter on one slice
4. Open jelly jar
5. Spread jelly on other slice
6. Put slices together
7. Enjoy your sandwich!

Notice how specific and ordered these steps are? That's what makes a good algorithm!
